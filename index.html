ABOUT US

Vision:

We live in a time where our data is used daily to influence our lives – what you see, how you vote, and what you believe. But this control does not belong in the hands of corporations or political actors. "Take Back Your Data" stands for a future where we all regain control over our personal data. A future where this data belongs to us, and we are free to decide how it is used. Together, we can create a digital world that is safe and free – for you, your family, and our entire society.

Mission

Today, our personal data is often used to influence us – what you see, how you vote, and what you buy. When a search engine shows you results that exactly reflect what you previously viewed on another site, or when an e-commerce shop recommends products based on your previous searches – it’s no coincidence. It is the result of targeted data processing designed to steer your decisions.

Join the Movement

Artificial intelligence and deepfakes are not just future concerns – they are already being used to manipulate content. Fake videos of prominent personalities are emerging, spreading extreme opinions and shaping what you believe. These technologies are already threatening the integrity of individuals, institutions, and even democracy. Why is there no solution yet? Many of the largest tech companies base their business models on the collection and use of data – and they won’t give up this power voluntarily. But if we act together, we can use these technologies to protect ourselves. Together, we can build a future where you have control over your data, and technology is used for the good of people. We have a choice: Do we use AI for manipulation or as a tool to protect and promote freedom? The technology that protects us is ready – and we have the know-how to apply it. We don’t just demand action – we act. Take the first step by joining our movement. Share our vision, get involved, and be part of the solution. Now is the moment to act and reclaim control. Why you must act now:

  • The danger is real and affects you personally: Every day, you are influenced by personalized ads, manipulated content, and fake videos. These technologies are already being used to control your opinions. Now is the time to do something about it.
  • There are solutions, but change requires all of us: The big tech companies have no interest in giving up control over data. They will do everything in their power to prevent this change. But together, we can drive this transformation.
  • Together, we can create a better future: Imagine a world where your data is safe and you have full control over your digital identity. This future is possible, and we know how to achieve it. Join us now and be part of the solution.
